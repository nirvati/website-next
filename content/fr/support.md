# Get Support

<div class="rounded-xl bg-white dark:bg-neutral-800 py-2 px-6">

If you need help while using Nirvati, there are several ways to get support:

- **Telegram chat**: Join our [Telegram chat](https://t.me/nirvati) to ask questions and get help from the community.
- **Email support**: You can reach out to us via email at [aaron@nirvati.de](mailto:aaron@nirvati.de).

We are working on more support options, including Discord, Matrix, a forum, as well as BlueSky, Mastodon and Nostr channels. Stay tuned for updates!
